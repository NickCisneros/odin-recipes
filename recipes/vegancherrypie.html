Vegan Cherry Pie

Description

This is a very simple, four-step, vegan cherry pie recipe.

Ingredients

  • Premade pie crust
  • 1 pound cherries
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Steps

  1. Smash cherries into a pulp.
  2. Warm cherries in a pot, mixing in 1 TBS of sugar at a time.
  3. Pour cherry mixture into pie crust.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes at 450F.
  5. Remove pie from oven, let cool for 25 minutes.
  6. Slice and serve.
