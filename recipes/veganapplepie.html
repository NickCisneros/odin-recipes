Vegan Apple Pie

Description

This is a very simple, four-step, vegan apple pie recipe.

Ingredients

  • Premade pie crust
  • Three apples
  • Cinnamon
  • Vegan butter

Steps

  1. Melt butter.
  2. Sautee apples in the butter until soft.
  3. Mix cinnamon into the apple sautee when finished.
  4. Pour mixture into pie crust.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes at 450F.
  6. Remove pie, let rest for 25 minutes.
  7. Slice and serve.
