Vegan Pumpkin Pie

Description

This is a very simple, four-step, vegan pumpkin pie recipe.

Ingredients

  • Premade pie crust
  • Pumpkin puree
  • Pecans
  • Vegan whipped cream

Steps

  1. Pour the pumpkin puree into the pie crust.
  2. Sprinkle pecans on top.
  3. Bake the pie for 45 minutes at 450F.
  4. Take pie out and let rest for 25 minutes at room temperature.
  5. Put whipped cream on and serve.
